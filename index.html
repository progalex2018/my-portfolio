ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in Puerto Rico 🇵🇷. Ever since I was a kid I've been fascinated by videogames. The first game I ever played if I recall correctly was Grand Turismo 3 in the PS2. Since then, videogames have become part of my life. It gave me the opportunity to be creative, to strategize, to love storytelling and most importantly, to have fun.

Later on, my interest for computers and electronics grew and had the opportunity in high school to enroll in a vocational curriculum which covered topics like computer repair, electricity and electronics. Reaching the end of high school, I applied for a computer science bachelor's in the University of Puerto Rico at Bayamon 🤠. I continued my journey until I graduated. Now, I am fully committed to keep making games and create ways for people to express themselves through them.
